How to Brew

Four steps to your perfect cup

01

Boil Fresh Water

Heat clean water to about 95°C — just before a rolling boil to preserve the herbs' natural goodness.

02

Place Your Tea Bag

Drop one Celie Tea bag into your cup or mug, then pour the hot water over it gently.

03

Steep for 5 Minutes

Let the botanicals bloom fully. The longer the steep, the richer and more healing the cup becomes.

04

Sip & Restore

Remove the bag, breathe in the warm aroma, and sip slowly. Best enjoyed morning or evening.
